TOM TURNER PORCELAIN
   
NOVEMBER, DECEMBER 2012 & JANUARY 2013 FIRING

Something old, something new, something blue, and a little gold. These three months I played with some ideas and put gold luster on old pots and new pots. Gold luster is pure gold fired on the glaze surface. If the surface is glossy, the gold is glossy. If the surface is matte or crackled, so is the gold. Then I did some porcelain/stoneware blend tests and fired them in both kilns. I also continued research on the Chemically reduced Copper Red Oxblood glazes so colors range from bright reds to maroons. I have noted the old website numbers I re-fired. As always, please ask questions.
