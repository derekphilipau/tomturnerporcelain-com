AUGUST 11 FIRING

All dimensions are height and width in inches, and all pots are made of "Turner Porcelain"

The crystalline glazes are new to me and as I start to use them, I am learning their likes and dislikes. They bubble, cause the body to bubble, and sometimes there might be a little pinhole in the glaze. These glazes are so incredible that I am accepting some of this, the really bad ones are destroyed. You will see some of these in the photos and if you have questions, please call or email. Gray spots are shadows and glare and reflections come with the territory(gloss). I hope you enjoy these as much as I do, because I think they are incredible. That's my feeling after 50 years of running glaze tests. Realize too that there are wonderful pots under these wonderful glazes.. Enjoy".
