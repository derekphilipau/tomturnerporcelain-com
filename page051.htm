This is it for September 2010 firings. These six pieces went through another refire and I feel improved them. When I refire, I chance loosing the piece because there is more melt, the opportunity for pin holes, small bloats, crazing, and glaze run off. Because of this refiring, I accept a few blemishes and try to point them out. At ANY TIME that you are not satisfied with a purchase, please return it insured and upon receipt of the piece in good condition, I will refund your purchase price. I realize that photographs can only do so much. Many times the work is better than the photographs can show. Thank you for your continued support. Tom
