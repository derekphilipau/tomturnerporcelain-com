Artist Professor Zhou Guo Zhen
Professor Zhou Guo Zhen is a National Treasure in China. We attended the Opening Ceremony of his Museum dedicated to him and his work in Anren, China, Hunan Province. The county government paid for and built the museum in Anren, his home town. Born to farmers, he spent his life studying, making, and teaching Ceramic Art. The faces of his sculpture capture the essence of each animal.
